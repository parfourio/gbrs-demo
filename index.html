Who We Are

Built From the Ground Up by Tier 1.

GBRS Group is unique. Our founding members are composed exclusively of Special Mission Unit Veterans — and our extended instructor network is drawn from retired SMU operators across the service branches.

We are obsessively committed to maintaining the highest standards of excellence. That commitment isn't a marketing line. It shows up in every piece of gear we design, every course we teach, and every program we build.

Our goal is 1% better every day — for ourselves and for the people who train, equip, and challenge themselves alongside us.
